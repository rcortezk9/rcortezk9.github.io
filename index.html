Responsiveness - you bet

Sites are built to accommodate all aspects of a mobile user experience, regardless of what kind of device they are using. These sites will work just as well on a mobile device as on a desktop, giving users access to the full content of a site and not a stripped down version of the main site. What you get is a seamless adjustment to the new dimensions of the user’s screen.

Agile Approach

Means managing the design and building of the website in a flexible, incremental way. Applying it to everything from software development and user experience to visual design and writing content. Since the approach is highly flexible, it can change and grow as new information and problems are encountered.
